About

I am a Ph.D. student at the University of Salerno at Software Engineering (SeSa) Lab.

Research Interests

My research explores the intersection of human factors and software engineering, with a particular focus on Risk Management in the Emergence of Community Smells and Socio-Technical Challenges in ML-Enabled Systems. I am passionate about investigating the human and organizational dimensions that influence software development processes, aiming to bridge the gap between technical and social considerations in modern software systems. My work seeks to uncover how socio-technical dynamics impact the quality, sustainability, and evolution of software systems, particularly in collaborative and machine learning-driven environments.
